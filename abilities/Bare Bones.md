# BARE BONES

> **Level 1 Valor Ability**  
> **Recall Cost:** 0

When you choose not to equip armor, you have a base Armor Score of 3 + your Strength and use the following as your base damage thresholds:

- **Tier 1:** 9/19
- **Tier 2:** 11/24
- **Tier 3:** 13/31
- **Tier 4:** 15/35
